About Me

Texas is where I call home. I grew up in the Dallas-
Ft. Worth area, and I currently live in San Antonio.
My interest in the tech industry started while I was
studying Government and History at the University
of Texas at Austin. I realized that I had more interest
in knowing how my computer worked as opposed
to using it to write all those research papers for
government classes. Nevertheless, I graduated
with a Bachelors of Arts in Government and History.
With the triumph of attaining my bachelors, I
embarked on a new journey to do what I really love;
Front End Development.

To Develop or not to Develop?
Now that is the question.

After designing my first website from
the ground up, and with a little
help from the lovely instructors
over at SkillCrush, I found my niche, and
I'm never turning back.
